ABOUT THE GAME

Skull Treasures is basically your classic match-3 game, but with this whole vibrant puzzle adventure thing going on.
Players must match three or more identical pieces to clear them from the board. The further you get, the more stuff they throw at you - new mechanics, obstacles, challenges. You really gotta start thinking ahead and making smarter moves.
It's got that perfect mix of brain-teasing strategy and really nice, colorful graphics that just feel good to play.
Every time you beat a level, you get to dig a little deeper into this treasure-packed world.
With hundreds of levels to solve, the adventure continues to grow as your skills improve.

A WORLD FULL OF PUZZLE CHALLENGES

  • There are over 1000 levels in this thing, and they're all different - different layouts, different goals, the works.

  • Some levels require clearing specific objects, while others challenge players to collect treasures or reach score targets. As you progress, puzzles become more complex and introduce new mechanics that require creative solutions.

  • The levels get tougher as you go, which keeps things interesting and makes you think harder about your moves.

POWERFUL BOOSTERS AND BONUSES

When you're really stuck on a hard level, the boosters can be a real lifesaver.

Special combinations unlock powerful effects that can clear multiple tiles at once, remove obstacles, or trigger chain reactions across the board.

Boosters add an exciting tactical layer to the gameplay, allowing players to solve puzzles in creative ways while maximizing their scores.

Honestly, figuring out how to use these bonuses right is usually what makes or breaks those really tough levels.
